Player Types

There are 4 types of players in the game: Standard, Trending, Featured and Legendary.

Standard

Players are based on their performance during the current season

Player development : Yes

How to sign:

  • eFootball™ Coins (Random)
  • GP (Nominated)
  • Chance Deal

Trending

Players are based on a specific match or week in which they put an impressive performance during this season

Player development : No

How to sign:

  • Nominating Contract

Featured

Hand-picked players that are depicted based on their performance during the current season

Player development : Yes

How to sign:

  • eFootball™ Coins (Random)
  • eFootball™ Points (Nominated)
  • Chance Deal

Legendary

Players are based on a specific season when they had a stellar performance (This also includes retired players who had a brilliant career)

Player development : Yes

How to sign:

  • eFootball™ Coins (Random)
  • eFootball™ Points (Nominated)
  • Chance Deal

How to Develop Players

Trending players are versions of players who have put in excellent performances on a particular week and have had their stats boosted to reflect that performance. This type of player is perfect for immediate inclusion on your team sheet, but can't be developed any further.

Standard, Featured and Legendary players can all be trained to boost their stats and fine-tune their abilities to fit your game plan. They may not start with impressive stats like their Trending counterparts, but given enough time to develop they may eventually become a better fit for your team!

Therefore, develop your players via Training; Fine-tune them to better suit your game plan and guide your team to glory!

Moreover, additional Training Programs will be introduced in the future to provide further development options for your players.

There are 5 types of matches as described below.

Challenge Event

Play against human opponents online, completing assigned mission objectives to earn rewards.

Play a casual online match against a human opponent.

Create an online match room and invite an opponent for a 1vs1 match.

eFootball™ Creative League

Use Creative Teams to play against the creme de la creme of eFootball™ World. Play PvP matches against evenly-matched opponents and gather points to rise the Divisions. You will gain rewards based on your performance during a Round (10 matches) and your final standing at the end of a Phase.
